Services

Our services cover the full spectrum of modern electrical engineering, from consulting through design and compliant system implementation.

01

Consulting

Comprehensive consulting throughout all project phases—from initial concept to execution. We focus on technically sound, cost-efficient and future-proof electrical solutions.

02

Electrical Design

Professional design of power and low-voltage systems for residential, commercial and public buildings. Our designs are code-compliant, execution-ready and well coordinated with other disciplines.

03

Electrical Installations

Planning and supervision of electrical installations with high quality and safety standards. Reliable, efficient and compliant implementation is our priority.

04

Lighting Design

Development of energy-efficient and standards-compliant lighting concepts for interior and exterior spaces, combining functionality, architecture and user comfort.

05

EV Charging Stations

Design and integration of electric vehicle charging infrastructure for private, commercial and public projects, including load management and grid connection concepts.

06

PV System Design & Installation

Comprehensive planning and installation of photovoltaic systems for sustainable energy generation, focusing on efficiency, feasibility and building integration.

07

Lightning Protection Systems

Code-compliant design and installation of external and internal lightning protection systems to safeguard people, buildings and technical equipment.

08

Fire Detection Systems

Professional design and implementation of fire alarm systems according to applicable standards, ensuring early fire detection and maximum safety.

09

Voice Alarm Systems

Design and installation of voice alarm systems for controlled evacuation in emergency situations, ensuring clear communication and reliable performance.

10

Access Control Systems

Design and integration of modern access control systems providing secure and flexible building access tailored to project-specific security requirements.
